  • • (May 7, 2013) Kennesaw State to graduate more than 2,400 students during spring commencement

    College of Science and Mathematics/Michael J. Coles College of Business – May 15, 2 p.m.

    Speaker: Shealynn Buck

    Shealynn Buck, M.D. is the executive director for Employee Health Solutions at DeKalb Medical in Decatur, GA.

    Prior to joining DeKalb Medical in 2012, she was owner of Prescription to Thrive, LLC, a firm specializing in health and wellness strategic consulting and communications services.

    Buck graduated magna cum laude from Kennesaw State University in 1996 with a Bachelors of Science in Biology. She received the Distinguished Alumni Award in 2008 and said that her undergraduate years at Kennesaw State fostered her interest and determination to pursue a medical degree. She attended Emory University School of Medicine and completed her residency and fellowship training in Pathology & Laboratory Medicine.

    Buck is a member of Kennesaw State's College of Science and Mathematics advisory board and the Michael J. Coles College of Business advisory board.
