The Apps made by Kennesaw State University's very own ACM Student Chapter
The ACM Student Chapter is an independently run student organization at Kennesaw State University dedicated to the technical profession of computer programming and app development. Down below you will find examples of some of the apps we have developed together. We are constantly working on new apps every week that we meet. Our goal is to improve our skills in programming for all the current platforms and have fun while doing it.

About

We are a certified student Organization dedicating ourselves to the advancement of Kennesaw State Computer Science.

Mission: To broaden the horizon of all CS Students through educating about and experimenting with computing machinery.

Description: The ACM offers a wide range of activities including application development and contest participation. We are currently seeking coders of all levels, web developers, and anyone with an interest in technology to join in on current and upcoming projects. Join today!
